Introduction

In ICM - Intrinsic Curiosity Module(Pathak et al., 2017), the novelty of the state is evaluated based on the prediction error of the forward dynamics model. However, the output of the forward dynamics model is not stable due to the rapidly changing encoder and thus reduces the state representation consistency. The previous states explored earlier by the agent suddenly become novel in the future due to the inconsistency in the encoderâ€™s representations, thus suffering from a significant performance drop. To address this problem, we propose a novel framework, the Momentum Curiosity Module(MCM).
